Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on the law, you have certain rights regarding your personal information that Edikt Media collects and processes:

  • Right of Access – You can request copies of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification – You can ask us to correct inaccurate data or complete incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure – You can request that we delete your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing – You can ask us to limit how we use your personal data in specific situations.
  • Right to Object – You can object to the processing of your personal data in certain cases.
  • Right to Data Portability – You can request that we transfer the personal data you provided to another organization or directly to you, when applicable.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent – If we rely on your consent to process your data, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details at the top of this Privacy Policy. If you have concerns about our use of your personal information, you can also make a complaint by emailing us at ediktmedia@gmail.com.
