STPI Conference Event

The STPI Conference brings together experts, researchers, and professionals to advance knowledge and innovation in toxicologic pathology

Overview

The STPI Annual Scientific Conference is a flagship event hosted by the Society of Toxicologic Pathology in India. It serves as a national platform for professionals, researchers, and students in the field of toxicologic pathology to come together and share knowledge. The conference features keynote speeches by leading experts, insightful oral and poster presentations, and interactive workshops focused on the latest scientific advancements and research methodologies.

Beyond knowledge exchange, the event fosters academic and industry collaboration, encouraging young researchers to showcase their work and engage with experienced professionals. With a strong emphasis on scientific innovation and community building, the STPI Conference continues to play a significant role in shaping the future of toxicologic pathology in India..

Client
Society of Toxicologic Pathology - India
Location
Bengaluru
