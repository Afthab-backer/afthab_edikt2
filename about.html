Get to know about Edikt Media

At Edikt Media, we craft immersive brand experiences that blend creativity, strategy, and technology. Headquartered in Pune, we are a multidisciplinary creative agency specializing in events & exhibitions, UI/UX, digital design, branding, and integrated media solutions.

We bring together a passionate team of marketing and sales professionals, developers, designers, strategists, and storytellers who work collaboratively to transform ideas into impactful visual narratives.

Our Origin Story

We are thinkers with creativity in our DNA—minds that see beyond the ordinary and speak volumes through design. In a world that’s constantly evolving and increasingly driven by visuals, we believe even the simplest ideas deserve to be seen in extraordinary ways.

Our founder envisioned a space where clarity meets creativity—a studio that cuts through the noise with ideas that are bold, fresh, and deeply impactful. From that vision, we set out to craft meaningful brand experiences that stand out in today’s cluttered digital and physical landscapes.

Every project we take on is a step towards that goal—telling stories with design, emotion, and purpose.
